My CrystalThai

Description

  • Author: Phong Van Nguyen

  • www.mycrystalthai.net

  • 27 March, 2020

A local restaurant that specializes in Thai cruisine.

Problem(s):

The restaurant couldn't communicate with their customers due to the lockdown order. They couldn't inform their customers about their irregular hours, menu changes, what the business is doing to help with the pandemic

Result(s):

  • 24/7 low cost advertising
  • Increased sales by at least 15% on average over the past few months
  • Doubled customer satisfaction level
